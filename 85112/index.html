what is 8511 films?

From a young age, Founder Tyelur Watkins has always enjoyed picking up a camera to capture moments. It started with skateboarding videos as a child, gradually expanding in scope and skill. At 18 Tyelur began creating content for local businesses. He soon realized he could serve individuals, businesses and organizations on a deeper, more meaningful level — thus came 8511 Films.

With the commitment to serving everyone to the highest level, 8511 Films has created content for a multitude of businesses. Ranging from top rated dog trainers, boxing programs, top-tier roofing companies to nationally recognized facilities.

At 8511 Films, our mission is to make your vision come to life. Our sole purpose is to capture everything — every moment, and create a masterpiece that exceeds your expectations. Whether you're looking to showcase your business, highlight a new product/service, put the spotlight on a testimonial/employee, or recap an event, 8511 Films will be there.
